Privacy Policy

Your privacy is a core principle of CipherPass.


1. No Data Collection

CipherPass does not collect, store, transmit, or log any passwords, generated values, or user input. Everything happens locally in your browser.


2. Local Processing Only

All password strength analysis and password generation are performed entirely on the client side using JavaScript. No network requests are made when using the Checker or Generator tools.


3. No Cookies or Tracking

CipherPass does not use cookies, analytics tools, tracking pixels, or any third-party scripts that monitor user behavior.


4. Third-Party Services

CipherPass does not integrate with third-party APIs or external services. The application is fully self-contained.


5. Security Responsibility

While CipherPass provides guidance on password strength and secure generation, the final responsibility for password usage and storage remains with the user. Always follow best security practices.


6. Changes to This Policy

If CipherPass evolves, this privacy policy may be updated to reflect improvements or new features. Any changes will remain aligned with our privacy-first approach.


Summary: Your passwords never leave your device. Ever.
